Ulamolohiya
A food guessing game

How to play
(1) Input a filipino dish
(2) Four main ingredients from the dish will show up
(3) Green ingredients mean the dish has the ingredient, Red means it doesn't have it

Pork
Soy sauce
Bay leaf
Vinegar

This means that the food has Soy Sauce and Bay Leaves but lacks Pork and Vinegar.

(4) You have seven tries to guess the food
Need some help...?
Note that adobo and sinigang are both pork, and nilaga is beef.
Also, ginataan is the squash kind, so there's no meat.

A strategy you can employ is to rule out the four main meats, being pork, beef, chicken, and fish.
You can also try ruling out various clusters of food like those predominantly with soy sauce or tomato sauce.
